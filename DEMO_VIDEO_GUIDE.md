## üé¨ **Recording Tips**

### **Tools:**

**Option 1: OBS Studio** (Best quality)
- Free, professional
- Download: https://obsproject.com
- Records screen + audio
- High quality output

**Option 2: Loom** (Easiest)
- Browser-based
- Quick and simple
- Website: https://loom.com
- Auto-uploads

**Option 3: Windows Game Bar** (Built-in)
- Press Win+G
- Click record
- No installation needed

### **Before Recording:**

1. **Close unnecessary tabs/apps**
2. **Set browser zoom to 100%**
3. **Clear localStorage** (fresh start)
4. **Prepare 2 MetaMask accounts:**
   - Account 1: "Alice"
   - Account 2: "Bob"
5. **Practice once** (dry run)
6. **Have script nearby** (but don't read it!)

### **During Recording:**

1. **Speak clearly and slowly**
2. **Show cursor movements**
3. **Pause briefly** after each action
4. **Point out key features** ("Notice this...")
5. **Explain as you go** (don't just click silently)
6. **If you make a mistake:** Keep going or restart

### **Audio Tips:**

1. **Use a good microphone** (or headset)
2. **Quiet environment** (no background noise)
3. **Speak with energy** (enthusiasm is contagious!)
4. **Smile while talking** (it shows in your voice)

### **Visual Tips:**

1. **Full screen browser** (hide bookmarks bar)
2. **Highlight cursor** (if tool allows)
3. **Zoom in on important parts** (optional)
4. **Keep it smooth** (no jerky movements)
